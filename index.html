Gardens


Ever wanted some peas and quiet? The Botanic Gardens might just be the place for you. The Gardens is developed along a Four-Core Concept. The four cores are Tanglin, which is the heritage core retaining the old favourites and charms of the historic Gardens; Central is the tourist belt of the Gardens; Bukit Timah the educational and discovery zone; and Tyersall-Gallop, the Learning Forest. From the Healing Garden, where you can find the Butterfly Pea Plant or the Bonsai Garden, where plants are carefully groomed to grow into aesthetically appealing shapes.The wide range of themes ensure everyone has something to look forward to at the Botanics Garden.


Acitivites to do


The Singapore Botanic Gardens is arguably the best place to have a picnic in Singapore. Fill your lungs with fresh air while enjoying a slice of sandwich and a bottle of chilled fruit juice could be enough to recharge you for the week ahead. This could also be an opportunity to catch up with your friends and family while enjoying the scenic beauty of the garden. If you prefer somewhere indoors, perhaps a trip down to Heritage Museum or the Botanical Art Gallery would interest you.
